My Trip to Vizag

It was one of the most refreshing experiences I've had. Here are a few places I visited:

Vuda Park

Perfect for a peaceful walk by the beach.

Kailasagiri

This hill park has a stunning view of the Bay of Bengal.

Thotla Konda

A peaceful place to learn about ancient Buddhist culture.

Top Experiences

  • Ropeway ride at Kailasagiri
  • Sunset at RK Beach
  • Street food near Vuda Park
